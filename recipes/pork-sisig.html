Pork Sisig

Description

Pork sisig is a popular Filipino dish. It can be considered as a main dish or an appetizer.

The composition of this dish is mainly of pig’s parts such as minced pork meat, ears, and face. Chicken liver, onions, and chili peppers are also added.

Ingredients

  • 1½ lb pork belly, boiled for 1 hour
  • ½ cup Mayonnaise
  • 2 pcs onion, chopped
  • 4 pcs Thai chili pepper, chopped
  • 2 pcs long green pepper, chopped
  • 16 g Knorr SavorRich Pork, equivalent to 1 sache
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1⁄4 cup margarine
  • 2 cup cooking oil

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a deep cooking pot or deep fryer. Fry the boiled pork belly until golden brown and crispy.
  2. Chop the crispy pork belly into small pieces. Set aside.
  3. Heat half of the margarine in a pan. Sauté half of the onions until soft. Add chopped pork. Saute for 30 seconds.
  4. Add Knorr SavorRich. Stir and then add chili peppers and season with ground black pepper and soy sauce. Cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add mayonnaise. Stir and add remaining onion. Set aside.
  6. eat a metal plate (sizzling plate) on a stovetop. Melt remaining margarine.
  7. Transfer the crispy pork sisig into the metal plate. Let it stay for 30 seconds.
  8. Serve with warm rice and your favorite beverage. Enjoy!
